About Us

BGCW-Bharathidasan Government College For Women is located in pondicherry and it is affiliated with Pondicherry University.The college is offering courses to the girls child for their development of mind and knowledge through research and training programmes and industrial convocations with the practicality of the education and imparting professional Skill.

Bharathidasan Govt College for Women is situated in Puducherry in Pondicherry state of India. Established in 1968, it is accredited from NAAC and it is affiliated to Pondicherry University. BGCW, Puducherry offers 17 courses across 6 streams namely Arts, Science, Paramedical, Vocational, Commerce & Banking, Management and across 6 degrees like BSc, BA, B.Com, M.Phil, M.Com. BGCW campus is spread over 5 Acres. Hostel facility is not available for its students. Additional campus facilities such as Auditorium, Canteen, Computer Lab, Cultural zone, Theatre, Library, Medical Facilities, Cls. Room, Sports, Grounds are also there.

Overview :




BHARATHIDASAN Statue

Statue of Bharathidasan Govt.college for women(Autonomous)



Bharathidasan Government College for Women, in the name of the great poet of this soil, was ushered into being in the beginning of the Academic Year 1968-1969. During the span of this 50 years the college has witnessed a phenomenal growth both qualitatively and quantitatively. It caters to the needs of women students by offering a wide range of Science, Arts and Commerce courses (i.e.) 16 Under-Graduate, two Post-Graduate and two M.Phil. and one Ph.D. Programme.

The excellent performance of the students both in the curricular and extra-curricular activities has facilitated the institution to get the autonomous status in the academic year 2004-2005. Autonomy extension is granted from 2012 to 2018. This college is the first autonomous institution in the Union Territory of Puducherry affiliated to the Pondicherry University.

With focus on the Knowledge-based economy, pioneering efforts are being undertaken to exhibit the talents of our students. Every year more than 200 students of this institution get themselves placed in suitable jobs through the Placement Cell.
Bharathidasan Government College for Women with its autonomous status will bloom as an epicenter in promoting quality standards and shaping a Knowledge-driven economy.
